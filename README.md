# Potion Of Immortality

The idea is simple. It adds an immortality potion. And it was actually pretty simple.
I used MCreator since it's the most user-friendly option.

## Requirements
* **Minecraft Version**: 1.21.8
* **Mod Loader**: NeoForge

## Installation
1. Download the mod [here](https://modrinth.com/mod/immortality-potion) (has yet to be reviewed; if it says 404 not found, go [here](https://www.planetminecraft.com/mod/potion-of-immortality/))
2. Drop it into your .minecraft\mods folder.
3. Launch the game with the mod!

## How to brew
You're gonna need a Brewing Table, Nether Wart, Water Bottles, Blaze Powder, and a Totem of Undying.
First, you wanna place the Brewing Table and put your Blaze Powder and Water Bottles in it.
Then, put in your Nether Wart to brew the water bottles into Awkward Potions.
With your Awkward Potions in there, you wanna put a Totem of Undying in there to brew it into the Potion of Immortality. And now you have brewed immortality potions!
